What is PrecisionPro?
PrecisionPro by Himanshu Gupta is an exponent calculator that calculates the power of a given base and exponent, providing any specified precision. It employs the Newton-Raphson method to compute roots accurately, accommodating real numbers, negative, positive, and decimals. Precision settings can range from 100 digits to thousands, limited by the device it runs on.
